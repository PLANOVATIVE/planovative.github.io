About Us

Planovative is a community and regional planning consultancy and business optimization firm serving communities across British Columbia. Our multidisciplinary team—comprising planners, designers, data scientists, web developers, and community engagement specialists—is dedicated to fostering sustainable, inclusive, and vibrant communities while helping businesses thrive through strategic planning, innovation, and data-informed solutions.

At the heart of our work is a strong belief in the power of data-driven decision-making and meaningful community engagement. By integrating advanced geospatial technologies with human-centered design, we help our clients plan strategically, set clear priorities, and make informed decisions that address local needs and support long-term sustainability.

Planovative’s innovative approach has supported nonprofits, community organizations, and continues to grow through ongoing collaboration with municipalities, provincial agencies, and public service organizations throughout British Columbia. We are proud to help our partners achieve their development goals while honoring local character, promoting equity, and protecting environmental integrity.

Areas of Expertise

Climate Risk, Social Vulnerability and Emergency Planning

Planovative leverages cutting-edge GIS (Geographic Information System) tools and climate data to evaluate risks and build resilient communities. We help municipalities and NGOs understand the intersection of climate impacts and social vulnerability. We offer planning solutions for urban safety and emergency preparedness.

Complete Communities and Official Community Plans (OCP) Updates

We support long-range planning by developing multi-criteria decision-making systems and updating Official Community Plans rooted in the principles of complete communities.

Social Service and Infrastructure Planning

Using spatial and statistical analyses, we evaluate access to critical services in underserved areas, supporting data-informed investments in community infrastructure.

Mobility & Active Transportation

We design evidence-based plans to promote integrated multimodal transportation networks—focusing on public transit, walking, and cycling—using cutting-edge geospatial techniques, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and computer vision approaches.Additionally, Planovative evaluates transportation systems, including paratransit and accessibility services, with an emphasis on equity and efficiency.

Suitability Analysis for Land Use, Housing and Business Development

We provide spatial decision-support services for identifying optimal locations for housing, tourism, industrial and business development.

Business Development and Marketing Strategies

We help businesses optimize their operations and growth strategies through a data-driven approach, leveraging social, environmental, economic, and market analytics to inform smarter, more sustainable decisions.
