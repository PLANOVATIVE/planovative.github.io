Overview

Build on what already works — and make your work easier to find.

Non-profits and charities are constantly creating value: reports, evaluations, frameworks, handbooks, and tools that rarely travel beyond local networks or a single project website.

TrueNetwork brings these materials together from across Canada so you can: avoid reinventing the wheel, learn from peers, and ensure your own work is visible to the people who need it.

Non-profit use cases

How non-profits use TrueNetwork.

Stop reinventing tools

Find existing guides, curricula, frameworks, and toolkits from organizations working on similar issues, and adapt them to your context.

Show your impact

Map your documents and programs to SDGs and themes so funders and partners can quickly see how your work contributes to broader goals.

Find peers & partners

Discover organizations in other regions or sectors tackling similar challenges and explore collaboration opportunities.

Strengthen proposals

Use peer examples and SDG-aligned evidence from TrueNetwork to support funding applications and concept notes.

Onboard new staff

Point new team members to curated community reports and toolkits so they can quickly understand the landscape and key practices.

Contribute to national learning

Share your reports and tools so other organizations can build on your work — and so your experience is visible in national conversations.

Key capabilities

What non-profits get with TrueNetwork.

Smart search across community knowledge

Search by topic, population, SDG, or geography to find relevant documents and organizations from across Canada.

Organization profile & visibility

Ensure your organization’s profile, documents, and focus areas are easy to find for peers, funders, and governments.

SDG analytics

See which SDGs your work contributes to and where there may be opportunities to expand or clarify your impact story.

Collaboration & funder maps

Explore networks of organizations and funders by theme to identify where you fit and who you might partner with next.
