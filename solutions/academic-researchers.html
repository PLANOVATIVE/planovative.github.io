Overview

A structured view of community practice and “gray literature”.

Much of the most relevant evidence for social policy, public health, housing, climate, and equity work never appears in peer-reviewed journals. It is embedded in reports, handbooks, evaluations, and toolkits produced by community-based organizations, governments, and networks across Canada.

TrueNetwork brings this community-generated knowledge into a structured environment with SDG tags, thematic lenses, and organizational context, helping academics and researchers locate, interpret, and connect with practice-based evidence.
