Overview

A trusted, unified view of your community partners.

Municipal teams rely on community organizations, yet often lack visibility into who is active locally, what they do, and how their work aligns with municipal priorities.

TrueNetwork integrates knowledge from 137,000+ Canadian charities, including organizational profiles, reports, guides, and collaboration networks — giving municipalities an evidence-based foundation for funding, planning, and strategy.
